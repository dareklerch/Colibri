FUNDRAISING

Deep tech innovation needs significant funding before reaching a commercially viable product. Investors and funding agencies have to believe in your potential before backing your high risk/high impact roadmap.

For this, you need to present them with a clear and compelling story. We help you craft that narrative based on the unique features of your technology and business model.

Whether you need an investor memorandum, a grant application, or a new pitch deck, we are ready to help your ideas shine. These documents have a wide selection of very specific target audiences: angel investors, venture capital firms, or funding agencies.

CONTENT
MARKETING

As a deep tech entrepreneur, you are bringing to the market something radically new. As such, you must take the initiative and teach people what your innovation is all about.

Providing informative and easily accessible content will help the ecosystem thrive and position you as a thought leader in the community.

We can help you create an engaging company blog, draft enlightening white papers and tutorials, and organise webinars with other industry leaders..

STAKEHOLDER
ENGAGEMENT

As your company grows, you will need to keep in touch with customers and partners. Effective engagement requires using different tools operating at various timescales—from rapidly-paced Twitter to more thorough newsletters.

We want your company to become one of the go-to sources of information in your domain. This will increase your chances of attracting new customers, top talent, and funding.

We can help you craft a long-term engagement strategy and operate your social media accounts.

PITCH
PREPARATION

The success of your enterprise often hangs on a five-minute pitch. You must condense years of hard work and ingenuity into a brief and memorable story. We know the stakes.

We can help you prepare and deliver that compelling pitch. We can support you along the way—from co-designing beautiful and informative slides to developing a clear and engaging script and prepping your team for a gruelling Q&A.
